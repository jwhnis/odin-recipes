Restaurant Style Mexican Rice

Description

A Mexican restaurant style rice that will remind you of your favorite Mexican restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup high quality long or short grain rice
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 large white onion
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • a total of 1 and 3/4 cups of water
  • 1-2 tablespoons corn oil

Steps

  1. Rough chop the onion and garlic.
  2. Place 1/4 cup water, tomato paste, onion, and garlic in a food processor and run until finely minced. Set aside.
  3. Place 1-2 tablespoons of oil in a large pan and heat on medium heat.
  4. Add 1 cup rice and cook until it smells slightly nutty, about 3-5 minutes. Be sure to stir constantly, otherwise it will burn.
  5. Add ingredients from the food processor and cook for 1 min.
  6. Add the remaining water, it should be 1 1/2 cups.
  7. cover and cook on lowest heat setting for 20 minutes.
  8. At the end of 20 minutes, lift lid to let the steam out and leave partially covered for 10 minutes.
  9. Fluff with a fork, serve, and enjoy!
